About Me

Here is a little bit about myself.

Welcome to my website! I'm a junior at Macalester College majoring in Computer Science and Economics. I was born and raised in South Korea, and I moved to the United States in 2015. In 2017, after my sophomore year, I went back to South Korea to serve in the Republic of Korea Army for 21 months. After my discharge in February 2019, I came back to the United States in Fall 2019 to resume my studies as a junior.

I am interested in software development, data analysis, statistical modeling, geospatial analysis, and consulting (management, economic, technology). Most of these are relatively new interests. I had not known that writing code to make a viable product, such as this website, can be so much fun! I wish to pursue a career that incorporates one or more of these to have a deeper understanding in what I enjoy doing.

Technologies: Python, Java, C, Git, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, R, STATA, SQL, Phaser, ArcGIS, S&P Capital IQ Platform, Microsoft Suite
Relevant Coursework at Macalester: Algorithm Design/Analysis, Software Design and Development, Statistical Machine Learning, Linear Algebra, Computer Systems Organization, Theory of Computation, Object-Oriented Programming, Core Concepts in Computer Science, Statistical Modeling, Multivariable Calculus III, Discrete Mathematics, Econometrics, Capital Markets
Online Coursework: Responsive Web Design (freeCodeCamp), Web Development 101 (The Odin Project), Intro to Databases and SQL Querying (Udemy), Spatial Data Science: The New Frontier in Analytics (Esri MOOC)
